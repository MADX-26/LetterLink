Grid Word

A strategic letter-placing word game for two players

How to Play
  • 1 Two players take turns placing single letters on any empty cell
  • 2 Words can be formed horizontally (left to right) and vertically (top to bottom)
  • 3 Points are scored based on valid words formed (minimum 2 letters)
  • 4 Only real words count for scoring - random letter combinations don't score
  • 5 The game ends when the grid is completely filled
  • 6 The player with the highest score wins!
